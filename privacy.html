Privacy Policy

Lil Dough · Last updated June 16, 2026

Your privacy matters. This policy explains how Lil Dough ("the app") handles your information. In short: the app collects nothing.

Data we collect

None. Lil Dough does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking.

Where your data lives

The counters, amounts, and close history you enter are stored only on your device. This data never leaves your device and is never sent to us or anyone else. Deleting the app removes this data.

Children's privacy

Lil Dough does not collect data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date.
